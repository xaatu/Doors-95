Entity Relationship Diagram

I designed this ERD to plan out a database on the subject of Old School Runescape NPCs and Items, linked by Location. Given that the game has over 10,000 items and probably another thousand or more NPCs, my aim was not to fill the whole database, but more to give an example and show how data could be retrieved and added.


While I had worked on databases before in a module at university, this was my first chance to create one from scratch, and I think being given the freedom to choose subject matter and source the data myself helped me grasp the concepts more clearly.

Items Table

The Items table features 118 items, labelled with Item_ID, Name, Description, Category_ID, Location_ID, Value, Weight, Rarity, and Level_Required.


Lower level items which were able to be sourced from locations rather than NPCs had specific Location_ID, whereas higher level items such as the ones shown in the screenshot, could have an NPC_ID column attached in future.

NPC Table

The NPC table features 14 NPCs, labelled with ID, Name, Description, Level, Location_ID, Is_Hostile, Weight, and NPC_Category.


The Is_Hostile column allowed me to use a Boolean value for the first time. I feel like this whole process gave me a better idea of how I may implement a database in future, perhaps in a game where items can be upgraded and NPCs can be interacted with.
